As a current student studying a bachelor's degree in I.T. at Otago Polytechnic, I am constantly working on learning new aspects of the industry.  From new frameworks to learning new computer languages,  I.T. does offer a lot of challenges.  

Working in the Engineering industry for 11 years, I decided to give I.T. a go.

I try my best in all aspects of life and believe in giving everything a go. I really enjoy being a dad to my two awesome girls who love keeping me on the go.

Being awarded Otago Polytechnic's most promising first year in 2016,  and best project  for the Dunedin Refugee Online Presence in 2017 was a great honor.

I have always enjoyed music but started djing  well over 15 years.  I dj all types of events from music artist tours, nightclubs, weddings and other functions.

 A highlight in my dj career was being the official opening dj act for the band Six60 and Tinnie Tempah in 2017 at Forsyth Barr Stadium to 5000 people.
